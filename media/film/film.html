TV Shows

Modern Family

Season 1 - Season 11

Synosis

Modern Family is a mockumentary-style sitcom that follows the lives of the eccentric yet lovable Pritchett-Dunphy-Tucker family. The series revolves around three interconnected households: the traditional but quirky Dunphy family, led by goofy dad Phil and no-nonsense mom Claire; the blended family of Jay Pritchett, his much younger Colombian wife Gloria, and her son Manny; and the same-sex couple, Mitchell Pritchett and Cameron Tucker, raising their adopted daughter, Lily. With witty humor, heartfelt moments, and hilarious misunderstandings, *Modern Family* explores the ups and downs of family life in the modern world. Through clever storytelling and relatable characters, the show delivers a fresh and comedic take on love, parenting, and the chaos of family dynamics.

Replay 1988

Season 1

Synosis

Reply 1988 is a heartwarming coming-of-age drama that follows the lives of five childhood friends and their families living in the same neighborhood of Ssangmun-dong, Seoul. Set in the late 1980s, the series beautifully captures the nostalgia of youth, the warmth of family bonds, and the deep friendships that stand the test of time.

Movies

Hachi: A Dog's Tale

2013

Synosis

Hachi: A Dog’s Tale is a heartwarming film based on the true story of Hachikō, the famously loyal Akita dog from Japan. The movie follows Parker Wilson, a kind-hearted professor who finds an abandoned Akita puppy at a train station and decides to take him home. Naming him Hachi, Parker and the dog develop an unbreakable bond.

To All The Boys I've Loved Before

2018

Synosis

To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before is a charming romantic comedy that follows Lara Jean Covey, a shy high school girl whose secret love letters—written to all the boys she’s ever had a crush on—accidentally get sent out. Suddenly, her quiet life is turned upside down as she faces the unexpected consequences of her private feelings becoming very public.
